# Citability Audit: Workplace & Real Estate Sector

**Audit Date:** 31 March 2026
**Scope:** Web citability analysis for 6 companies in workplace/tertiary real estate sector
**Methodology:** Web fetch and search-based analysis; only verified data scored

---

## Executive Summary

This audit evaluates the digital citability (credibility and verifiability online) of 6 workplace/real estate consulting firms across 5 dimensions:
- **Entity Clarity** (brand/category definition)
- **Claims + Evidence** (case studies, proprietary data)
- **Third-Party Citations** (press mentions, external validation)
- **Freshness** (content publication frequency/recency)
- **Multilingual Reach** (language availability and depth)

**Key Findings:** Parella and CBRE excel in proprietary research (barometers, surveys). Tetris-db leads in project portfolio documentation. Colliers and JLL show strong research but less recent French-specific content. Kardham demonstrates growth but limited proprietary data visibility.

## Scoring Methodology

### A) Entity Clarity (/10)
- **9-10:** Explicit category definition + clear positioning statement on homepage + consistent cross-site
- **7-8:** Clear positioning but slightly generic or embedded in longer copy
- **5-6:** Category implied but not explicitly stated; requires inference
- **<5:** Vague or unclear positioning

### B) Claims + Evidence (/10)
- **8-10:** 5+ case studies with numbers + proprietary research (barometer/survey) + published data
- **7-8:** 3-4 case studies + proprietary research OR strong market data
- **5-6:** 1-2 case studies + generic/inherited research
- **<5:** Claims without public evidence; no case studies/data visible

### C) Third-Party Citations (/10)
- **8-10:** 10+ press mentions + trade press + social proof (awards) + quoted in industry reports
- **7-8:** 5-10 press mentions + some trade coverage + minimal awards
- **5-6:** 2-4 press mentions + limited external validation
- **<5:** <2 mentions or only internal self-promotion visible

### D) Freshness (/10)
- **8-10:** Blog/news updated monthly or quarterly; latest content <3 months old
- **7-8:** Content updated quarterly; latest <6 months old
- **5-6:** Content updated 2-3x yearly; some dated material
- **<5:** Content >1 year old or static

### E) Multilingual (/10)
- **9-10:** 3+ languages with equal depth; consistent content across versions
- **8-9:** 2 languages (usually EN/FR/ES) with full content parity
- **6-7:** 2 languages but unclear depth; some sections may be translation-only
- **N/A:** Unable to verify language infrastructure
